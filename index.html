About

The fundamental adjoint triple:

$$\Sigma_f \dashv f^* \dashv \Pi_f$$

For weakening π: Γ.A → Γ:

$$\Sigma_\pi \dashv \pi^* \dashv \Pi_\pi$$

Where:

  • $$f^*$$ is substitution (pullback)
  • $$\Sigma_f$$ is dependent sum (left adjoint)
  • $$\Pi_f$$ is dependent product (right adjoint)

\(\Sigma_f \dashv f^* \dashv \Pi_f\) represents the adjoint triple.

Based in Chicago, US and Todmorden, UK
